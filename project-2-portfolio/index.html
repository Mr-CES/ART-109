About me

2D drawing has been a passion of mine ever since childhood. Those very old drawings used to consist of media I liked when I was a kid and that tradition still stays true to this day. I wear my inspirations on my sleeve, as evidenced by the fact of how much visual art I create based on other media, which stylistically bleeds into my original work. I create works on topics such as religion and the occult and how that bleeds into society as a whole. Even how some of these deities of these pantheons try to hide and blend themselves into our human society, to observe and possibly serve through the world of Joyful Nightmares.

The idea of religion also comes to mind. As a man of faith, understanding the values of religion is very important, but also coming to terms with the twisting of said religion. That is something to be explored through the world of The Spark of Creation.

Exploring these ideas through 2D digital art is my goal. Moving on from fan creations from media that I enjoy, to now exploring my own world and themes. I don’t intend to stop fan creations, only to move onward and expand, create more original works. Something I am able to call my own. Explore the darker themes I have always been interested in, but have never explored. Shed some new insight based on my own knowledge and experiences, despite my more positive exterior.

My videos are more for fun though, playing into that positive exterior. I record with friends and edit it to play into the lightheartedness of our attitudes. There’s no meaning other than that, just a group of friends having a good time via a game where we can connect and throw our ridiculous jokes and performances.
